The Sail Operator is a community project launched by Red Hat to build a modern operator for Istio. First announced in August 2024 , we are pleased to announce Sail Operator is now GA with a clear mission: to simplify and streamline Istio management in your cluster. Simplified deployment & management The Sail Operator is engineered to cut down the complexity of installing and running Istio.
